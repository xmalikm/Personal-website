Who Am I

About me

Hello, I'm Martin Malík

I'm a PHP fullstack developer, which means I'm able to create an entire website (backend & frontend) from scratch. I started my journey as a web developer at the Slovak University of Technology in Bratislava back in 2013, where i later got a master's degree in applied informatics.

As for backend development, I specialize in PHP frameworks Nette & Laravel and database systems PostgreSQL & MySQL. Code is tested using PHPUnit. At frontend, i use tools and libraries such as HTML, CSS (Sass, Bootstrap), javascript (jQuery, Vue.js), etc...

I consider myself a patient, well-organized person with analytical thinking and a high attention to detail. In my spare time I'm a big fan of sports of any kind, especially football. I like to watch a good movie or hang out with my family and friends. Also, I'm the father of a seven-month-old son Max.
